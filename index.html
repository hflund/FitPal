Training Guidelines

Superset Strategy

  • Exercises marked 2a/2b are supersets
  • Do set of 'a', rest 30s, do 'b', rest 60-90s
  • Pairs non-competing muscle groups

Progression

  • Add weight when completing all sets at top range
  • Compounds: +2.5-5kg increases
  • Accessories: +1.25-2.5kg increases

Rest Periods

  • Compounds: 2-3 minutes
  • Accessories: 60-90 seconds
  • Supersets: 30s / 60-90s between rounds
